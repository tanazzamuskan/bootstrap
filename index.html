About Us

Manav Rachna University is a leading State Private University (established vide Haryana State Legislature Act No 26 of 2014 & under Section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956), and recognized by UGC, offering globally relevant education. The University has evolved from Manav Rachna College of Engineering (MRCE), which was established in the year 2004; with a Motto of Creating Better Human Beings, who are globally competitive and socially responsible. With its striving vision and distinctive methodology, University received QS I Gauge Overall Diamond rating with a Platinum rating in Academic Development & Employability and a Diamond rating in Teaching Learning Process, Faculty Quality, Facilities and Social Responsibility.
